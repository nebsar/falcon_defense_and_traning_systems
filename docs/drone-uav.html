Drone & UAV Systems

State-of-the-art unmanned aerial vehicle platforms for reconnaissance, surveillance, and tactical operations in contested environments.

Overview

Falkon Aviation's Drone & UAV division designs, manufactures, and integrates cutting-edge unmanned aerial systems for military, law enforcement, and critical infrastructure protection applications.

Our platforms range from tactical micro-UAVs for close-range reconnaissance to medium-altitude long-endurance (MALE) systems capable of sustained operations over vast operational theaters.

  • Small tactical UAS with EO/IR payloads for infantry support

Key Technologies

Autonomous Navigation: GPS-denied flight capability with advanced inertial navigation, terrain-following radar, and AI-powered obstacle avoidance for operations in contested electromagnetic environments.

Sensor Fusion: Multi-spectral imaging payloads integrating electro-optical, infrared, synthetic aperture radar (SAR), and signals intelligence (SIGINT) sensors in a single platform.

Command & Control: Encrypted data links with anti-jam capabilities, satellite communication relay, and seamless integration with existing C4ISR architectures.
