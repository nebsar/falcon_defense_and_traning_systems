More Than a STEM Classroom

Falkon Aviation & Aerospace Innovation Laboratories are complete, school-ready learning environments designed for primary, middle, and high school students. Simulation, experimentation, engineering, coding, and real-world aviation scenarios come together in one integrated space.

Students do not simply learn about technology—they use it to plan, build, test, fly, communicate, and improve. The result is an aviation mindset built on curiosity, discipline, situational awareness, teamwork, and confident decision-making.

Three Learning Pathways

Each laboratory is configured around student age, educational goals, available space, and the level of technical depth your school wants to offer.

Pathway 01

Aviation Discovery Lab

Primary School // Ages 6–10

A highly visual, story-led introduction to flight that turns natural curiosity into structured discovery.

  • How aircraft fly
  • Simple aerodynamics experiments
  • Paper aircraft design challenges
  • Introductory mini-drone activities
  • Guided VR flight experiences
Pathway 02

Flight Academy Lab

Middle School

Mission-based learning connects science and mathematics with the tools and decisions used in aviation.

  • Flight simulation and procedures
  • Drone programming
  • Weather and navigation
  • Air traffic control fundamentals
  • Team flight challenges
Pathway 03

Professional Aviation Lab

High School

A career-oriented environment for advanced flight, engineering, autonomous systems, and geospatial applications.

  • Aviation ground-school foundations
  • Advanced multi-screen simulation
  • FPV and autonomous flight systems
  • Mission planning, GIS, and photogrammetry
  • Aerospace engineering and AI

One Lab, Multiple Aviation Domains

A modular ecosystem lets schools combine the domains that best match their academic vision, student profile, and future growth plans.

SIM

Flight Simulation

Configurable training stations for general aviation, commercial flight, and advanced mission scenarios.

UAS

Drones & Autonomous Systems

Mini drones, FPV, fixed-wing, and VTOL platforms support programming, operations, safety, and mission design.

ENG

Electronics & Engineering

Hands-on work with microcontrollers, onboard computers, autopilot systems, sensors, servos, and flight controllers.

AI

AI & Mission Intelligence

Age-appropriate projects introduce computer vision, object detection, mission planning, and intelligent flight systems.

AERO

Aerospace Design

Wind-tunnel experiments, wing design, aerodynamics, propulsion concepts, and introductory rocketry.

ATC

Air Traffic Control

Tower, radar, ADS-B, and radio communication activities develop awareness, precision, and coordinated decision-making.

XR

Immersive Learning

VR hangar, walk-around, maintenance, and emergency scenarios make complex environments accessible and memorable.

MRO

Aircraft Systems & Maintenance

Training components introduce motors, propellers, servos, speed controllers, inspection, and system integration.

Built for Lasting Educational Impact

Students move from passive learning to active participation: they plan, test, observe, communicate, make decisions, evaluate outcomes, and try again. Aviation becomes a practical platform for STEM learning, technical literacy, teamwork, leadership, and responsible risk awareness.

For schools, the laboratory becomes a visible center of innovation—supporting curriculum enrichment, project-based learning, clubs, summer programs, and student challenges while creating a distinctive experience for families and the wider community.

A Platform That Can Grow

Start with the pathway that fits your current goals, then expand with additional simulator stations, advanced drone platforms, aerospace experiments, geospatial tools, or new curriculum modules as student participation grows.
